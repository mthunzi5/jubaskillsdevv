About the Program

Why Join Juba Consultants?

Juba Consultants is a leading professional services firm committed to developing the next generation of talented professionals. Our internship program is designed to provide meaningful experience and mentorship to high-potential individuals.

We believe in investing in talent and creating an inclusive, supportive environment where interns can learn, grow, and contribute to real projects that make an impact.

Job Placement

Ready to Apply?

We're looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our internship program. If you meet the requirements and are passionate about professional development, we'd love to hear from you!

What We're Looking For
  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a tertiary qualification (University or TVET)
  • Strong communication and problem-solving skills
  • Professionalism and reliability
  • Willingness to learn and take on challenges
  • Not currently enrolled in any SETA (Sector Education and Training Authority) program
Required Documents
  • Copy of National ID or Passport
  • Recently certified qualifications (certificates, transcripts)
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Affidavit from police station (Commission of Oath) stating you are not under any SETA program
  • Any other supporting documents (optional)

The application process is simple and takes about 15 minutes to complete.
